Colorado’s Leader in Lease/Purchase and Rent To Own Options

Owning a home is truly the American Dream. Buying a home can be a difficult process, especially for those with poor credit, no credit, and/or a limited amount to invest. A Lease to Own, also known as Rent to Own, option may be just for you!

Advantages of working with WCS Property Solutions

Customized Solutions

We have a variety of different options available to provide the best solution to your unique situation. We will work closely with you to find an option that best fits your situation.

Transparency

We will always be clear and upfront about our entire process. We believe that it does not matter what we can do for you if you don’t understand what it is we are doing. We want everyone we work with to be 100% confident and comfortable with the decision they are making.

Honesty

This is an important trait that every business should have but unfortunately lack. At WCS we pride ourselves on being honest with you every step of the way. We are a business, so yes, we are going to make a profit but not at your expense. We provide Win-Win solutions for all parties.

NO Hidden Fees or Commissions

WCS is not going to nickel and dime you throughout the process. If you are a Seller, we will pay you your equity 100% in full. NOTHING extra is charged to you.

No Inspections Required

We do not require inspections to buy your home. In some situations, we may decide to have an inspection, but it is not to beat you up about the price or condition. It is just for our knowledge moving forward.

Hassle Free

Our process is easy. We will not bother or harass you to make a decision. First, we will show you the different ways you can sell your home and we will work with you to find the best option that will allow you to maximize your profit. Once you decide that one of our options is a good fit, the process is streamlined and quick.

We are Always Buying Homes

Regardless of market conditions we are always buying properties. We are not fly by the night or opportunity buyers. We buy homes in any condition and any market for full market value.

Largest Pool of Buyers

We are continually adding families to our list of Buyers looking for an opportunity to buy one of our homes. We have the experience and processes to ensure every family that moves into one of our homes crosses over the finish line.

Professional & Legal

We regularly work with Real Estate attorneys to ensure that we are following and keeping up to date with all local, state and federal housing laws. All of our agreements have been approved by our Real Estate Attorneys and every transaction is completed with the oversight of one of our Attorneys.
